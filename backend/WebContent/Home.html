About SAMYAK

SAMYAK is one of the biggest National Level Techno-Management fests in Andhra Pradesh with a flow of more than 20,000 students from all around the Nation, which has events designed to inspire and inform. Not just these, we have some amazing performances to enthral and entertain on the days of the event, there will be various events and cultural conducts.

  • Benefit in improving knowledge.

  • Multiple events and cultural programs during fest
